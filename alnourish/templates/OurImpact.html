    Why Algae?

    While the world is struggling to solve the problem of food sustainability by finding ways to feed the growing population by optimising the existing produce, we approached the problem from a more fundamental perspective and explored new avenues, resulting in an unconventional yet fundamentally natural solution to not only satisfy our food requirements but also make the global population healthier in a manner that's good for our planet. Touted as the 'food of the future' We aim to bring into the limelight a species of edible algae - Spirulina which has the potential to be in everything you eat in the next ten years.

    What The Experts Are Saying

    Hyped by supplement enthusiasts as providing a number of magical benefits, this superfood may be one of the most nutritious foods known to man and is deemed food of the future. Algae benefits are so pronounced — due to their high concentrations of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and proteins — that they are commonly referred to as superfoods.
    Easy to grow and densely packed with protein and nutrients, these organisms can make our food system more efficient and less resource-intensive.


    We, at ALNourish believe that the issue of unsustainable global food system and food security issue can be tackled immediately and potentially resolved forever by an unconventional yet fundamentally natural solution - Introducing Spirulina and other forms of algae in our lives as a nutritious, sustainable and scalable food source.
