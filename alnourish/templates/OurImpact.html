    What The Experts Are Saying

    Hyped by supplement enthusiasts as providing a number of magical benefits, this superfood may be one of the most nutritious foods known to man and is deemed food of the future. Algae benefits are so pronounced — due to their high concentrations of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and proteins — that they are commonly referred to as superfoods.
    Easy to grow and densely packed with protein and nutrients, these organisms can make our food system more efficient and less resource-intensive.
