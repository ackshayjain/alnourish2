    The ALNourish Story

    Kicking the M out of Malnourish

    ALNourish is an initiative founded by five engineering undergraduates who are deeply concerned about the widely overlooked issue of food sustainability, or the lack thereof.

    We live in a world where one billion people go to bed hungry everyday and even more are malnourished. While we are unable to properly feed the existing population, what haunts the food enthusiasts is the fact that this population would rise to 9 billion people by 2050 and we would lack the sufficient amount of resources to feed the next generation. The issue of food security is made worse by the dwindling natural resources and already strained healthcare systems.

    Be it the malnourished kid in the poorest regions of the world or the average working millennial who wants fresh food for himself and his family, we strive to empower them all and change the way we eat, using algae as a nutritious, sustainable and scalable food source.

    Our Mission

    We are focused on incorporating algae into the human food chain and the global food system, making it mainstream.


    We, at ALNourish believe that the issue of unsustainable global food system and food security issue can be tackled immediately and potentially resolved forever by an unconventional yet fundamentally natural solution - Introducing Spirulina and other forms of algae in our lives as a nutritious, sustainable and scalable food source.
