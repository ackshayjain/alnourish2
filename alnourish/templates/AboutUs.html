    Kicking the M
    out of MalNourish!

    ALNourish is a startup founded to help eradicate malnourishment and poverty in Rural Indian Households.

    • While the world is struggling to solve the problem of food sustainability by finding new ways to feed the entire population with the existing produce, we explored new avenues and came up with something unconventional yet so basic which would not only satisfy our current needs but also provide additional health benefits. We bring into the limelight an algae - Spirulina which might as well be the food of the future and has the potential to be in everything you eat in the next ten years. Easy to grow and densely packed with protein, these organisms can make our food system more efficient and less resource-intensive.
    • Algae benefits are so pronounced — due to their high concentrations of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and proteins — that they are commonly referred to as superfoods.
